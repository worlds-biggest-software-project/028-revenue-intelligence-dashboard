## Market Research

- **Market Size**: Global Revenue Intelligence Platform market valued at $1.2B in 2024; projected to reach $3.5B by 2033 (12.8% CAGR).
- **Growth Drivers**: AI-powered analytics adoption, sales efficiency pressures post-macro slowdown, remote sales team visibility needs, forecast accuracy improvements.
- **Key Buyer Personas**: VP of Sales, Sales Operations leaders, Revenue Operations teams, Enterprise sales directors, CFOs (forecasting focus).
- **Pain Points**: Sales cycles lengthening (multiple stakeholders), forecast accuracy (50-70% typical), deal slippage, visibility into pipeline quality, coaching effectiveness.
- **Pricing**: Enterprise annual contracts ($50K-$500K+), usage-based (conversation minutes), seat-based models. Consolidation (Clari-Salesloft merger) shifting to bundled platform pricing.
- **Market Events**: Clari-Salesloft merger (December 2025) combining forecasting with engagement; Microsoft Dynamics 365 launching 10 autonomous sales agents (November 2024); every major platform adding agentic capabilities.

## AI-Native Opportunity

- **Autonomous Deal Analysis**: AI agent analyzing conversation recordings, emails, and CRM updates to auto-populate pipeline stages, flag at-risk deals, and estimate close probability.
- **Predictive Revenue Forecasting**: ML model learning from historical sales cycles to predict quarter-end revenue with confidence intervals; flagging forecast risks early.
- **Intelligent Sales Coaching**: LLM analyzing sales conversations to identify coaching gaps (objection handling, discovery quality) and suggesting specific improvements with recorded examples.
- **Deal Progression Prediction**: Agentic system predicting likely next steps in sales cycle, recommending actions, and automating routine follow-ups.
- **Multi-Signal Deal Intelligence**: Fusion of conversation data, web signals, buying intent signals, and organizational changes to surface hidden deal risks/opportunities.
